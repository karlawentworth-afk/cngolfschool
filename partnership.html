The Proposal

A new women's golf product for Costa Navarino, delivered by the only team in golf who can.

Costa Navarino is already the European destination for serious golf. Four signature courses, year-round sun, the calibre of facilities that attracts the world's leading sports brands. Mouratoglou Tennis sits inside the Westin. Anazoe Spa stands beside it. There is one significant gap. Women's golf.

Ladies Love Golf has spent over fifteen years coaching women exclusively, and has built the only psychological profiling system for golf in the world. We are proposing a partnership with Costa Navarino to deliver The Navarino Golf School for Women across all four hotels in the 2026 summer season. A pilot to begin, and a longer-term branded academy alongside Mouratoglou should the pilot prove the model.

How It Works

Each side does what it does best.

— Step One

We bring the guests

For the Three Day School we own marketing and recruitment. We use our LLG community and our Golf Breaks partnership to fill cohorts. All bookings flow through Golf Breaks, our usual travel partner. No reduced rate is requested from Costa Navarino.

— Step Two

You provide the stage

Costa Navarino provides accommodation, half-board, course access and a classroom space (per requirements below). We can deliver across any of your four hotels. Complimentary half-board rooms for the three coaches during delivery is the only commercial concession we ask for.

— Step Three

You market the One Day

For the One Day Intensive, you have the reach to your in-resort guests that we cannot match. We provide the marketing creative and materials. You promote through concierge, app, in-room and reservations. An admin share applies if you manage the booking.

What's In It For Costa Navarino

Eight reasons this is commercially compelling.

Shoulder-season occupancy

Schools run June, September and October. Exactly the weeks Costa Navarino needs to fill. Our demographic actively prefers cooler weather for golf.

Standard rate accommodation

All guest bookings flow through Golf Breaks at standard rates. No reduced rate is requested from us. Costa Navarino books rooms at full margin.

Upgrades and accompanying guests

Many of our guests will choose to upgrade their room. Many will travel with partners or friends. All accompanying guest revenue flows entirely to Costa Navarino.

Extended stays

A meaningful number will arrive earlier or stay on after the school. Each extra night is incremental room and F&B revenue at no marketing cost to you.

An academy alongside Mouratoglou

Costa Navarino is the destination where serious people come to get better at things. Our school slots into that brand architecture as the women's golf authority. The only such academy in Europe.

High ancillary spend

Our guests are exactly the right customer for Anazoe Spa, fine dining, cultural experiences and wine programmes. Wellness-focused professional women aged 45 to 65 with disposable income to spend.

Repeat visits and referrals

Cohorts become friendship groups. Improving golfers come back to play. Our ninety-day post-school programme keeps the conversation alive and turns one stay into many.

Format expansion potential

Once the women's model is proven, the same framework can be deployed for men, beginners, juniors or low handicappers. Whichever gap Costa Navarino most needs to fill next.

What We Need From You

The hosting requirements are modest.

The school is designed to slot into resort operations with minimal additional complexity. Everything below is needed only on school delivery dates.

  • Coach hospitality
    Complimentary half-board rooms for the three coaches during delivery. Coaches' families welcome when accompanying. This is the only commercial concession we ask for.
  • Classroom space
    A private meeting room for ten people, available for morning sessions across each school. Natural light, screen or projector, comfortable seating.
  • Course access
    Reserved tee times for the cohort on the Dunes Course or Bay Course on delivery days. Green fees are paid by guests as part of their school package.
  • Practice facilities
    Range, short game area and putting green access for the cohort, upon agreement. Standard practice rates apply, paid by guests as part of the school package.
  • Meals
    Breakfast and dinner included in the half-board element of guest packages. Graduation dinner at a property restaurant of the hotel's choosing.
  • Marketing reach for the One Day
    Inclusion of the One Day Intensive in Costa Navarino's guest communications, app, in-room materials and reservations conversations. Cross-promotion to existing guests at all four properties.

Beyond Women's Golf

2026 is the proof point. Then we scale.

Once the women's school model is proven at Costa Navarino, the same framework can be deployed for other underserved segments. The psychological profiling system works for any golfer.

The Beginners School

For complete newcomers to the game. A confidence-led entry into golf, addressing the largest growth segment in the sport. Strong fit with the Westin's family positioning.

The Junior Academy

For young golfers aged 12 to 18. Complements Mouratoglou's existing junior model. Family-driven destination demand for school holiday periods.

The Men's Intensive

Same methodology, same coaches, same profiling system, redesigned for men. Strong fit with existing Costa Navarino guest demographics.

The Low Handicapper School

For golfers handicap 18 or below seeking elite-level personal coaching. Premium positioning, smaller cohorts, higher price point. Aimed at the Mandarin Oriental guest.
