The Proposal

A new women's golf product for Costa Navarino, delivered by the only team in golf who can.

Costa Navarino is already the European destination for serious golf. Four signature courses, year-round sun, the calibre of facilities that attracts the world's leading sports brands. Mouratoglou Tennis sits inside the Westin. Anazoe Spa stands beside it. There is one significant gap. Women's golf.

Ladies Love Golf has spent over fifteen years coaching women exclusively, and has built the only psychological profiling system for golf in the world. We are proposing a long-term partnership with Costa Navarino to deliver The Navarino Golf School for Women across all four hotels in the 2026 season. A new branded academy alongside Mouratoglou, drawing a new and high-value guest segment to the resort.

What's In It For Costa Navarino

Eight reasons this is commercially compelling.

Shoulder-season occupancy

Schools run April to June and September to October. Exactly the weeks Costa Navarino needs to fill. Our demographic actively prefers cooler weather for golf.

Standard rate accommodation revenue

All guest accommodation, food and beverage is paid directly to Costa Navarino at standard rates. No negotiated discount required. Three Day School at the Romanos alone could deliver €416k in resort revenue.

Extended stays

Many guests will arrive earlier or stay on with partners and family. Conservatively half will add nights. Across the season this is meaningful additional room nights and F&B spend.

An academy alongside Mouratoglou

Costa Navarino is the place where serious people come to get better at things. Our school slots into that brand architecture as the women's golf authority. The only such academy in Europe.

Cross-promotion to a new audience

Ladies Love Golf reaches over ten thousand women in the UK with an industry-leading 89% email open rate. Plus our partnership with Golf Breaks, the world's largest golf travel operator. Costa Navarino is presented to an audience it cannot easily reach directly.

High ancillary spend

Our guests are exactly the right customer for Anazoe Spa, fine dining, cultural experiences and wine programmes. Wellness-focused professional women aged 45 to 65 with disposable income to spend.

Repeat visits and referrals

Cohorts become friendship groups. Improving golfers come back to play. Our 90-day post-school programme keeps the conversation alive and turns one stay into many.

Format expansion potential

Once the women's model is proven, the same framework can be deployed for men, beginners, juniors or low handicappers. Whichever gap Costa Navarino most needs to fill next.

What We Need From You

The hosting requirements are modest.

The school is designed to slot into resort operations with minimal additional complexity. Everything below is needed only on school delivery dates.

  • Guest accommodation
    Up to 8 rooms across the chosen hotel for each Three Day School cohort. Standard rates apply, paid by guests directly. No special rate to LLG required.
  • Coach hospitality
    Complimentary half-board rooms for the three coaches during delivery. Family of coaches welcome when accompanying. This is the only commercial concession we ask for.
  • Classroom space
    A private meeting room for ten people, available for morning sessions across each school. Natural light, screen or projector, comfortable seating.
  • Course access
    Reserved tee times for the cohort on the Dunes Course or Bay Course. Practice facilities for two hours per morning. Standard green fees, paid by guests.
  • Meals
    Breakfast and lunch included in guest packages. Graduation dinner at a property restaurant of the hotel's choosing. All paid at standard rates.
  • Marketing reach
    Inclusion of the school in Costa Navarino's guest communications, app, in-room materials and reservations conversations. Cross-promotion to existing guests at all four properties.

Beyond Women's Golf

The 2026 season is the proof point. Then we scale.

Once the women's school model is proven at Costa Navarino, the same framework can be deployed for other underserved segments. The psychological profiling system works for any golfer.

The Beginners School

For complete newcomers to the game. A confidence-led entry into golf, addressing the largest growth segment in the sport. Strong fit with the Westin's family positioning.

The Junior Academy

For young golfers aged 12 to 18. Complements Mouratoglou's existing junior model. Family-driven destination demand for school holiday periods.

The Men's Intensive

Same methodology, same coaches, same profiling system, redesigned for men. Strong fit with existing Costa Navarino guest demographics.

The Low Handicapper School

For golfers handicap 18 or below seeking elite-level personal coaching. Premium positioning, smaller cohorts, higher price point. Aimed at the Mandarin Oriental guest.
