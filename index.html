The School

Tour-level coaching, designed for women.

Every great golfer has a mental game coach. Now you do too — wrapped inside three days of personalised, intensive transformation at one of Europe's most exceptional resorts.

The school is built on a simple truth: most women don't need a better swing. They need to understand how their psychology shows up on the course, then receive a personal strategy to play their best golf consistently. We've spent over a decade coaching women, and we've identified 49 distinct psychological profiles — yours is one of them.

From the moment you arrive, you are coached as an individual. Two PGA professionals. A printed workbook personalised to your profile. Skills stations that target where you actually lose shots. A signed 90-day commitment that takes your handicap down — and keeps it there.

The Profiling System

Forty-nine ways of sabotaging a perfectly good round. One of them is yours.

Before you arrive, you complete a 30-question profile. From your answers, we identify a primary and secondary psychological pattern — the way your mind tends to interfere with your golf. Every workbook, every coaching session, every piece of feedback is built around your specific combination.

The Three Days

Discovery. Skills. Commitment.

I Discovery & Foundation

You meet yourself, properly

Your profile is revealed in a personalised workbook. A mental game masterclass. A course management session. Then nine holes where we watch you play, take notes, and start to understand exactly where your shots are going.

II Systems & Skills

You learn where you actually lose shots

A custom scorecard built around you. Short game decoded. Skills stations targeting bunkers, approach, putting and short game — each one coached to your profile. A 15-minute one-to-one with our lead coach. Six holes putting it all together.

III Commitment & Graduation

You sign for a better golfer

Course management built around how you actually play, not how a scratch golfer plays. A full 18-hole graduation round. A signed 90-day commitment. A celebration dinner with individual presentations. A WhatsApp group that keeps you honest until handicaps officially drop.
